# Privacy Policy — Z Dev Toolbox

_Last updated: 2026-06-13_

## English

Z Dev Toolbox is a browser extension that provides a set of offline developer
utilities (JSON formatting, encoding/decoding, hashing, QR code generation,
and similar tools).

**We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.**

- The extension runs entirely inside your browser. All processing happens
  locally on your device.
- The extension makes **no network requests** to any server. Any text you paste
  into a tool never leaves your machine.
- The `storage` permission is used **only** to save your tool preferences and
  configuration (for example, your last-used tool and formatting options) in
  your browser's local storage. This data stays on your device and is never
  uploaded.
- We use **no analytics, no tracking, no cookies, and no third-party services.**

Because no data is collected or transmitted, there is nothing for us to store,
access, or disclose.

### Contact

For questions about this policy, open an issue at:
https://github.com/Goalonez/z-dev-toolbox

## 简体中文

Z Dev Toolbox 是一个浏览器扩展,提供一组离线开发者工具(JSON 格式化、编码/解码、
哈希、二维码生成等)。

**我们不收集、不传输、不出售、不共享任何个人数据。**

- 扩展完全在你的浏览器内运行,所有处理都在本地设备上完成。
- 扩展不向任何服务器发起网络请求,你粘贴到工具中的任何文本都不会离开你的设备。
- `storage` 权限仅用于在浏览器本地存储中保存你的工具偏好和配置(例如最近使用的
  工具、格式化选项),这些数据仅保留在本地,绝不会上传。
- 我们不使用任何统计分析、追踪、Cookie 或第三方服务。

由于不收集或传输任何数据,我们没有任何数据可以存储、访问或披露。
